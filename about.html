About Florida English Academy

On the auspicious day of 1st April 2010, Florida English Academy was established to provide education to students. The credit goes to respected Adiba Saoban who worked shoulder to shoulder with us to give a leading shape to this institution what it is today.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide easy and perfect education to all. We believe in fostering a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and critical thinking, preparing students to become innovative leaders and compassionate global citizens.

At Florida English Academy, we are guided by core values that shape our educational philosophy and daily interactions. These values include:

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching, learning, and personal growth.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, respect, and ethical conduct in all endeavors.
  • Community: Building a supportive and inclusive environment where every member feels connected and valued.
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity, new ideas, and continuous improvement.
  • Compassion: Nurturing empathy, kindness, and a commitment to serving others.
